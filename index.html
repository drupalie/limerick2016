The Drupal Ireland Association are pleased to invite everyone to Drupal Limerick 2016. Meet and learn from the best Drupal minds in Ireland bringing together developers, themers, end users and those interested in learning more about the platform.

About the event

Drupal is a powerful and flexible content management system powering some of the most popular sites in the world. This open-source software is built and maintained by a growing community of users and developers. The Drupal Ireland Association holds two annual events around the island, and this May it’s coming to Limerick!

The event will be supported by Johnson & Johnson at the IDC building, based in the UL Campus. The riverside campus is an ideal location for visitors and locals wishing breath fresh air and have a nice walk around, there is plenty of restaurants and bars open to public too.

Highly recommended coming by bike or walking, also it’s easily accessible by public transport or car.

Who is this for? The event will be about developing with Drupal, bringing together developers, themers, end users and everybody interested in learning more about this powerful and flexible framework backed by one of the world’s most innovative open source communities.
